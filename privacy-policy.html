Privacy Policy for MLetras

Effective Date: December 17, 2024
Last Updated: January 2025

Quick Summary: MLetras respects your privacy. We collect your email address for account creation and authentication. Your folders, bookmarks, and notes are stored securely on our servers. We don't collect location data, contacts, or share your data with third parties except for providing lyrics through MusixMatch API.

Introduction

MLetras ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application MLetras (the "App").

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Email Address: Required for account creation and authentication via one-time password (OTP)
  • Username: Optional display name you choose for your account
  • Search Queries: Song titles and artist names you search for
  • User Preferences: Display settings, language preferences, and app customization choices
  • Bookmarked Songs: Songs you save to your favorites or organize in folders
  • Folders: Custom folders you create to organize your bookmarks
  • Notes: Personal notes you create related to songs or artists
  • Search History: Your recent searches for convenience (stored locally)

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Account Information: User ID, subscription status, account creation date, last login time
  • Session Data: Authentication tokens for maintaining your login session
  • Note: We do not collect detailed usage analytics, device identifiers, or crash reports. Basic account information is collected solely for app functionality and account management.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Name: We do not collect your full name or real name
  • Phone Number: We do not collect your phone number
  • Location Data: We do not access or collect your location information
  • Contacts: We do not access your contacts or address book
  • Photos/Media: We do not access your photos, videos, or other media files
  • Microphone/Camera: We do not access your microphone or camera
  • Payment Information: All subscription payments are processed by Apple through App Store, we never see your payment details

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  1. Account Management: Create and manage your user account, authenticate your identity using email and OTP
  2. Provide Core Services: Display song lyrics and search results
  3. Sync Your Data: Synchronize your folders, bookmarks, and notes across devices
  4. Subscription Management: Manage your Pro subscription status and features
  5. Improve User Experience: Remember your preferences and search history
  6. App Functionality: Enable bookmarking, folder organization, notes, and settings
  7. Technical Support: Diagnose and fix technical issues
  8. App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve the App
  9. Security: Protect your account from unauthorized access

Third-Party Services

MusixMatch API

  • We use MusixMatch API to provide song lyrics and metadata
  • Your search queries are sent to MusixMatch to retrieve lyrics
  • We do not share your personal information (email, account data) with MusixMatch
  • Only search queries (song titles and artist names) are sent to MusixMatch
  • MusixMatch's privacy policy applies to their services: https://www.musixmatch.com/privacy

Cloudflare Services

  • We use Cloudflare Workers and Cloudflare D1 database to host our backend services
  • Your account data and user content are stored on Cloudflare's secure infrastructure
  • Cloudflare Workers act as a proxy for API requests, helping protect your privacy by keeping API keys server-side
  • Cloudflare's privacy policy applies: https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/

Apple App Store

  • For Pro subscriptions, payments are processed by Apple through the App Store
  • We receive transaction information from Apple but never see your payment details
  • Apple's privacy policy applies to payment processing: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

Data Storage and Security

Cloud Storage (Backend Servers)

  • Account Information: Your email address, username, subscription status, and account metadata are stored securely on Cloudflare D1 database
  • User Content: Your folders, bookmarks, and notes are stored on our backend servers to enable synchronization across devices
  • Authentication Data: Session tokens and OTP codes are stored temporarily for authentication purposes
  • Data Location: All backend data is stored on Cloudflare's secure infrastructure

Local Storage (Device)

  • Search History: Stored locally on your device for quick access
  • User Preferences: Display settings and app preferences stored locally
  • Cached Data: Some data may be cached locally for offline access and performance
  • Session Token: Stored locally to maintain your login session

Data Security

  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information
  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Authentication uses secure one-time password (OTP) system
  • Session tokens are securely generated and expire after 24 hours
  • Backend databases are secured and access-controlled
  • We follow industry-standard security practices

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties, except:

  1. Service Providers: Trusted third parties who assist in operating our App (MusixMatch, Cloudflare)
  2. Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  3. Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

Your Rights and Choices

Account Management

  • Account Deletion: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data through the App settings. This will delete your email, username, folders, bookmarks, notes, and subscription data. This action cannot be undone.
  • Data Access: You can access your account data through the App interface
  • Data Portability: You can export your data by accessing it through the App

Data Control

  • Clear Local Data: You can clear all locally stored data (search history, preferences) through the App settings without deleting your account
  • Delete Content: You can delete individual folders, bookmarks, or notes at any time
  • Reset Preferences: You can reset all app preferences to default settings

Privacy Settings

  • You can manage your account settings through the App
  • You can clear search history at any time
  • You can manage your subscription through the App Store settings

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

International Users

If you are accessing our App from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Providing notice through the App if significant changes are made

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Your account and associated data (email, folders, bookmarks, notes) are retained until you delete your account
  • Session Tokens: Authentication tokens expire after 24 hours of inactivity
  • OTP Codes: One-time password codes expire after 10 minutes and are automatically deleted
  • Search History: Stored locally on your device until you clear it or uninstall the App
  • User Preferences: Stored locally until you reset them or uninstall the App
  • Deleted Accounts: When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: support@mletras.com
Website: https://mletras.com/privacy

Subscriptions and Payments

  • Pro Subscription: MLetras offers a Pro subscription with additional features
  • Payment Processing: All subscription payments are processed by Apple through the App Store
  • Subscription Data: We store your subscription status and transaction IDs (provided by Apple) to manage your Pro features
  • Subscription Management: You can manage or cancel your subscription through the App Store settings or within the App
  • Restore Purchases: You can restore previous purchases if you reinstall the App or use it on a new device

Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • Apple App Store requirements
  • Google Play Store requirements
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
