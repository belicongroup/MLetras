## Step 1: Create Subscription Group (YOU ARE HERE)

**In the "Reference Name" field, type:**
```
MLetras Subscriptions
```

**Then click "Create"**

---

## Step 2: Create Your Subscription

After the group is created, you'll see a "+" button or "Create Subscription" button.

**Click it and fill in:**

1. **Reference Name:**
   ```
   MLetras Pro Monthly
   ```
   (Internal name - only you see this)

2. **Product ID:**
   ```
   com.mletras.pro.monthly
   ```
   (Must be unique - format: your.bundle.id.pro.monthly)

3. **Subscription Duration:**
   Select: **"1 Month"**

4. **Click "Create"**

---

## Step 3: Set Pricing

1. Click on your newly created subscription
2. Go to **"Pricing and Availability"** tab
3. Click **"Add Price"** or **"Set Price"**
4. Select: **$6.99** per month
5. Make sure **USD** is selected
6. Click **"Save"**

---

## Step 4: Add Subscription Information

1. Go to **"Subscription Information"** tab
2. Fill in:

   **Display Name:**
   ```
   MLetras Pro
   ```
   (This is what users see)

   **Description:**
   ```
   Unlock unlimited folders, unlimited notes, auto-scroll lyrics, dark theme, and priority support. Cancel anytime.
   ```

3. Click **"Save"**

---

## Step 5: Set Up Free Trial

1. Go to **"Subscription Duration"** or **"Introductory Offers"** section
2. Click **"Add Introductory Offer"** or **"Set Up Free Trial"**
3. Select: **"Free Trial"**
4. Duration: **14 days**
5. Click **"Save"**

---

## Step 6: Add Localization (Optional but Recommended)

1. Click **"Add Localization"** or go to **"Localizations"** tab
2. Select: **English (U.S.)**
3. Fill in:
   - **Name:** `MLetras Pro`
   - **Description:** 
     ```
     Unlock unlimited folders, unlimited notes, auto-scroll lyrics, dark theme, and priority support. Cancel anytime.
     ```
4. Click **"Save"**

---

## Step 7: Review and Save

1. Review all your settings
2. Make sure everything looks correct
3. Click **"Save"** on the subscription
